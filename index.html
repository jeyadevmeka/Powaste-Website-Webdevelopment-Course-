What is Waste Management?

The term "waste management " refers to all of the tasks necessary to control waste, from the point of collection to recycling and monitoring. Waste, as used in waste management, is any undesirable or useless substance created by human activity and can take many different forms. Waste can be solid, liquid, or gas, and each has a different manner of managing it and a different way to dispose of it.

Reducing the hazardous consequences of such garbage on the environment and human health is the goal of waste management.Municipal solid waste, which is produced by industrial, commercial, and home activity, makes up a significant portion of waste management.
